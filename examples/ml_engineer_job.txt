Machine Learning Engineer - AI-First Healthcare Platform

Company: HealthAI Technologies
Location: Boston, MA (Hybrid) or Remote

About HealthAI Technologies:
HealthAI is revolutionizing healthcare through cutting-edge artificial intelligence. Our platform helps healthcare providers make better decisions, improve patient outcomes, and reduce costs. We're processing millions of patient records daily and need talented ML Engineers to help us scale our AI capabilities.

The Role:
We're seeking a Machine Learning Engineer to join our core AI team. You'll work on developing and deploying machine learning models that directly impact patient care. This role combines cutting-edge ML research with practical engineering to build reliable, scalable systems that healthcare providers trust with their patients' lives.

Core Responsibilities:
• Design, develop, and deploy machine learning models for clinical decision support
• Build scalable ML pipelines processing millions of healthcare records in real-time
• Implement deep learning models for medical image analysis and natural language processing
• Collaborate with clinical experts to ensure models are medically accurate and useful
• Optimize model performance for low-latency inference in production environments
• Develop monitoring and alerting systems for model performance and data drift
• Conduct rigorous testing and validation of models to ensure safety and reliability
• Work with cross-functional teams including data engineers, clinicians, and product managers
• Stay current with latest ML research in healthcare and implement relevant advances

Required Qualifications:

• Experience deploying ML models to production at scale
• Solid understanding of machine learning algorithms and statistical methods

• Knowledge of ML ops best practices and tools (MLflow, Kubeflow, or similar)
